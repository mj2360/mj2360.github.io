Francesca Woodman

About The Artist

American, 1958-1981

Woodman is a photographer well known for black-and-white self-portraits. She often explored sexuality and the body, through recurring motifs such as mirrors, birds, and skulls . Woodman's parents were artists and she had been taking pictures since she was thirteen. She produced over 800 untitled prints while working as a photographer, despite her career ending with her suicide at the age of 22.
